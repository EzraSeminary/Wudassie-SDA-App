# Wudassie App - App Store Description

## Short Description (80 characters)
Complete Amharic hymnal collection for worship with lyrics, sheet music, and audio.

## Full Description

**Wudassie** is a comprehensive worship app designed for the Seventh Day Adventist community, featuring complete collections of Amharic hymnals and Hagerigna songs. Whether you're leading worship, studying hymns, or simply enjoying spiritual music, Wudassie provides everything you need in one beautifully designed app.

### Key Features:

📖 **Complete Hymnal Collection**
- Access the full Seventh Day Adventist Amharic Hymnal collection
- Browse and search through hundreds of worship songs
- Quick number search to find songs instantly

🎵 **Hagerigna Songs**
- Dedicated collection of Hagerigna hymns
- Easy navigation and search functionality
- Complete lyrics for every song

❤️ **Favorites**
- Save your favorite hymns for quick access
- Build your personal worship playlist
- Never lose track of your most-loved songs

🎼 **Sheet Music**
- View sheet music for supported hymns
- Swipe through multiple pages
- Perfect for musicians and worship leaders

🎧 **Audio & Music**
- Access audio recordings for hymns
- Integrated YouTube music links
- Listen while you read along

🎨 **Customizable Experience**
- Adjustable font sizes for comfortable reading
- Dark mode for low-light worship settings
- Beautiful, modern interface designed for worship

🔍 **Smart Search**
- Quick number-based search with intuitive numpad
- Find any hymn instantly by its number
- Smooth, modern bottom sheet interface

### Perfect For:
- Worship leaders and musicians
- Church congregations
- Personal devotion and study
- Anyone who loves Amharic worship music
